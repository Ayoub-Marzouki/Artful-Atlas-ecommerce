Who are we?

Artful Atlas is an online platform on the surface. Deep inside on the other hand, it's a vibrant community dedicated to showcasing the rich Moroccan artistry to the world.

Our mission is to amplify the essence of Moroccan identity by spotlighting emerging talents who skillfully capture the essence of their heritage through their distinctive artistic expressions.
Ever pondered the possibility of communication transcending linguistic barriers? We've discovered a universal language; that is art. Through the powerful medium of artistic expression, we break down language barriers and connect individuals from diverse backgrounds on a profound level.

Join us in celebrating the boundless communication and understanding fostered by the language of art. Through our curated selection of artwork, we aim to bridge cultures and foster a deeper appreciation for the beauty and diversity of Moroccan art.

Our team is passionate about supporting young Moroccan artists on their journey to global recognition. We provide a platform for these talents to shine, offering them exposure and opportunities to showcase their work to a global audience. Join us on our journey as we celebrate the creativity, innovation, and cultural richness of Morocco, one masterpiece at a time.

Why us?

  • Supporting young artists: We are passionate about supporting young Moroccan artists (and that is our top priority) on their journey to global recognition. We provide a platform for these talents to shine, offering exposure and opportunities to showcase their work to a global audience.

  • Amplifying Moroccan identity: Our mission is to showcase the essence of Moroccan identity by highlighting rising talents who are able to make you taste heritage and traditions of Morocco simply through their painting brushes.

  • Bridging cultures: Through our careful selection of artwork, we aim to bridge cultures and promote a deeper appreciation for the beauty and diversity of Moroccan art. Join us in celebrating the rich tapestry of Moroccan culture.

  • Celebrating creativity and innovation: Join us on our journey as we celebrate the creativity, innovation, and cultural richness of Morocco, one masterpiece at a time. Together, let's explore the endless possibilities of Moroccan artistry.

Our mission and values

Our mission is deeply rooted in promoting Moroccan artistry and cultural heritage to the world. We hold these core values close to our hearts:


  • Empowering artists: We empower artists by offering them exposure, opportunities, and resources to grow and thrive in their artistic journey, ensuring that their voices are heard and their talents are recognized globally.

  • Celebrating Moroccan identity: We are committed to showcasing the diverse and vibrant artistic expressions that capture the essence of Moroccan culture and heritage.

  • Fostering creativity and innovation: We believe in nurturing and supporting emerging talents, providing them with a platform to express their creativity and share their unique perspectives with the world.

  • Promoting cultural exchange: Through our selection of artwork, we strive to foster cross-cultural dialogue and appreciation, bridging gaps and fostering understanding among individuals from diverse backgrounds.

  • Community-driven approach: We believe in the power of community and collaboration. We aim to cultivate a supportive and inclusive community where artists and art enthusiasts come together to share their passion for art and creativity.
